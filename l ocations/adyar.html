ESTATE HISTORY

Adyar

Where the River meets the Elite.

The "Clogged River" Legacy

Named after the **Adyar River**, this neighborhood transformed from 17th-century British hunting grounds into Chennai's most prestigious pin code. The establishment of the **Theosophical Society in 1883** put Adyar on the global map as a center for philosophy and calm.

Famous Landmarks

  • Theosophical Society: A 260-acre green lung with a 450-year-old Banyan tree.
  • Kalakshetra: The cradle of Bharatnatyam, founded in 1936.
  • Elliot's Beach: The cleaner, quieter alternative to Marina.

Real Estate Pulse

Adyar is home to "Gandhi Nagar," Chennai's first planned layout. Today, it commands property values nearly **4x higher** than North Chennai, making it the ultimate destination for luxury villas and high-end apartments.
