MODERN CORRIDOR

OMR

The 45km lifeline of New Chennai.

From Forest to Fortune

Once a desolate metal road known as the "Old Mahabalipuram Road," this stretch was a favorite for 1980s action movie shoots. Everything changed in **2000 with TIDEL Park**, triggering a massive vertical explosion of glass towers and luxury townships.

The Tech Giants

OMR (Rajiv Gandhi Salai) is the workplace for over **1 million professionals**. It houses headquarters for TCS, Amazon, and Cognizant, turning former villages like Navalur and Sholinganallur into mini-cities.

Infrastructure 2026

With the **Phase 2 Metro** and the **Elevated Expressway**, OMR remains the #1 choice for investors looking for high-rental yields and gated community lifestyles.
